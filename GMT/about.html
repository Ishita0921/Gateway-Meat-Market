about us

We are an independently owned and operated meat market, that began its fresh start in April of 2008. We guarantee one-hundred percent satisfaction, not only in our products but in our customer service as well.

Whether you are trying to economically feed a family of five, or have an upcoming barbeque to plan for, our staff are there to help you in every way possible. We have acquired many loyal, satisfied customers from all over Dartmouth, Halifax, Bedford, Sackville and beyond, and we hope to add you to our growing family of clientele!
